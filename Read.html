THE STORY

Eren and Ousman were stewards of a rich master. Some day accusations about Eren and Ousman were brought in that they were wasting their master's goods.
He called both stewards and asked them to account for their stewardship. Both stewards realized that their jobs were in danger.

Both stewards formulated a plan. Eren called all of the master's debtors and asked them each how much they owed the master. He then asked each to reduce their quantity of debt quickly.
Meanwhile Ousman looked for someone to take the fall rather than himself.

When called back to the master for their stewardship accountability, the master commended the shrewdness of Eren and condemned Ousman for trying to deceive him.
Both still lost their stewardships but Eren fell into the favor he earned himself from the master's debtors. Ousman though was subjected to forced labour for his deceitful actions.

THE LESSON

MATTHEW 10:16
"Behold, I send you out as sheep in the midst of wolves. Therefore be shrewd as serpents and harmless as doves."

Jesus encourages us to be shrewd,wise and harmless in His ministry. Both deceitfulness and shrewdness have similarities, one of them being self preservation.
Being deceitful means to intentionally mislead or trick someone through dishonesty or cunning.
Being shrewd is to be sharp and clever. To be able to judge people's intentions and act accordingly.
As Jesus sent us out he asked us to be not only shrewd but harmless too. The lord hates lies. The moment your shrewdness invovles harming the other party through a lie or deception then it is no longer being shrewd but deceitful.
That is the main difference. Does your shrewdness harm someone? Then you are being deceit. And those who are deceit will be driven into forced labour.

PROVERBS 12:24
The hand of the diligent will rule, but slothfulness/deceitfulness will only lead to forced labour.
Be shrewd in His Kingdom and harmless as a dove. Do not deceive people for the LORD hates a lying tongue.
